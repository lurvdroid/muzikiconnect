About Us

Founded in 2016 and headquartered in Nairobi, Muziki Connect is a vibrant and diversified entertainment company dedicated to delivering the freshest and most trending news from the African entertainment scene. We are passionate about showcasing the dynamic world of Kenyan music and beyond, serving as your ultimate source for breaking stories, exclusive updates, and in-depth coverage.

Our team is made up of young, creative, and dedicated writers who work tirelessly to bring you accurate, up-to-the-minute information. We understand the pulse of the entertainment industry and aim to keep you connected to everything happening—from music releases and artist spotlights to viral trends and cultural moments.

Muziki Connect reaches a broad and engaged audience both locally and internationally through major digital platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, and our own website. This multi-platform approach ensures that our content is accessible wherever you are, whenever you want it.

We are committed to being the go-to destination for African entertainment lovers—where you find inspiration, excitement, and the stories that matter. At Muziki Connect, entertainment is more than news; it’s a celebration of culture, creativity, and connection.
