Sauti Sol's LADIA album at 10!!!

Sauti Sol is currently celebrating the 10th anniversary of their iconic album "Live and Die in Afrika" , which was originally released on November 21, 2015.

The band has initiated a week-long campaign to celebrate the album's impact, using the hashtag #LADIAat10. As part of the celebrations, their publishing company is working on an Afrohouse remix edition and reimagined tracks featuring a new generation of artists.

Fans have also been invited to participate in a cover series by recreating their favorite songs from the album, which includes hits like the title track "Live and Die in Afrika," "Sura Yako," "Isabella," "Nerea," and "Kuliko Jana".

Earlier in 2025, Sauti Sol marked the fifth anniversary of their fifth studio album, Midnight Train, which was released in June 2020. This album has achieved significant success, garnering over 500 million streams across various digital platforms and becoming the most streamed Kenyan album to date.

The band is set to reunite to perform at Sol Fest 2025 in December to celebrate this album milestone.
