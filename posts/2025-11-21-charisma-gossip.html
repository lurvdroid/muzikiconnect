Singer Charisma acquires luxury car!

Charisma, born Fidel Eli Shammah Omusula, popularly known as "Luhya Heat," is an Afro-pop, R&B, and contemporary fusion artist based in Nairobi. He first made waves in the Kenyan music industry as part of the acclaimed boy band Le Band, renowned for their exceptional harmonies, live instrumentation, and a modern sound that brought a new vibe to the East African boy-band scene. With hit tracks like “Number 1” and “For You,” the group quickly gained widespread recognition, establishing themselves as one of Kenya’s leading vocal acts. Following his successful tenure with Le Band, Charisma embarked on a solo journey, embracing the chance to express his unique musical identity. Charisma celebrated his new Mercedes Benz E-Class, revealing that his music career has brought him both "pain and joy".
